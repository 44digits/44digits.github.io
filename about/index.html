About Me

10 Oct 2019
1 min read

My name is Andrew and I’m a data analyst living in southern California.
I use python, statistics, and work with a number of geospatial tools.

I love discovering insights by bringing together and analyzing disparate data sets. My experience includes working for companies and organizations from multiple sectors. Tough environments where these were based include: Afghanistan, Kenya, Pakistan and Sri Lanka.
I primarily develop in Python but also apply: R, ArcGIS and other GIS tools. My college degrees in Psychology and Computer Science help me to work closely with clients and partners. In particular, my strengths lie in translating customer information requirements into actionable analysis and superior data solutions.
